About me

I am Sanjay Mythili, a Data Scientist with a strong passion for machine learning, data analysis, and engineering. Currently pursuing my MS in Data Science at the University of Colorado Boulder, I thrive on solving complex problems through predictive modeling, big data technologies, and cloud computing.

My interests span computer vision, natural language processing, and generative AI, along with data analysis, visualization, and data engineering. I enjoy working with large-scale datasets, building efficient data pipelines, and optimizing AI-driven solutions for real-world applications in healthcare, automation, and sustainability.

I have also contributed to AI research, publishing studies on computer vision, autonomous systems, and hyperspectral imaging in reputed journals, including Computers, Materials & Continua (CMC), and IEEE Xplore. My research focuses on deep learning, transformer architectures, and their applications in medical imaging and environmental monitoring.
